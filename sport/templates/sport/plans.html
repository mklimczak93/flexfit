Common questions

Short answer, thousands of places. Tap here to find classes and appointments at the best studios, salons and spas near you.

FitFlex members have access to special rates at the top studios, salons and spas. Not only can you save more with a FitFlex membership, you have the flexibility to use your credits where and how you want to.

After signing up, you’ll be able to see the credit price for a class or appointment, which varies by things like popularity, time and reservation type.

Good news, you can always add more! Just go to the “Add credits” tab in the app to purchase more and keep up your routine.

Don’t sweat — your credits roll over when you are on a paid FitFlex membership! Once you become a paying member of FitFlex, you can roll over up to the number of credits in your next month’s plan. For example, if your next month’s plan includes 19 credits, you can roll over up to 19 credits into that month, and they’ll appear in your account up to 48 hours after renewal. Any additional credits you have will expire at the end of the month and if you decide to cancel, all remaining credits will be forfeited on the last day of your membership.If you are on a trial, you will have access to your free credits until your trial end date. After this date, any leftover credits from the trial will expire. If you purchased any top-up credits or credit bundles during your trial, they will roll over into your next month.

You’ll be able to see each reservation’s cancellation window when booking (it’s usually 12 hours). If you cancel a reservation in time, we won’t charge you, and the credits you used to book will automatically be returned to your account. If you cancel late, you’ll receive a late cancellation fee.

If you miss a reservation without cancelling, you’ll get your credits back and be charged a missed reservation fee.

Although we’ll be sad to see you go, you’re easily able to cancel your membership at any time by following these steps. You can also cancel your trial at any time, and we’ll remind you of your renewal terms 2 days before it ends.
